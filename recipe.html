How to Make Kachori

Learn how to make crispy and spicy Kachori, a delicious snack that is perfect for tea-time or as a street food treat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup All-purpose flour
  • Â½ cup Semolina
  • 1 tsp Caraway Seeds
  • 1 tbsp Ginger Paste
  • 1 tsp Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Mix all-purpose flour, semolina, caraway seeds, and a pinch of salt in a bowl. Add water and knead into a smooth dough. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. For the filling, heat oil in a pan, add ginger paste and spices. Add some cooked potatoes or lentils if desired. Cook until aromatic.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls, roll them out, place the filling in the center, and seal them.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and deep fry the Kachoris until golden and crispy.
  5. Serve hot with chutneys or as-is!
